About Me

Highly motivated student in the pursuit of a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a concentration in Cybersecurity and a minor in Finance. Excellent problem-solving skills with a history of producing in high-intensity environments and demonstrated passion in the ever-evolving and fragile field of cybersecurity. First-generation background with established qualities of perseverance, grit, and determination in the face of adversity alongside indomitable optimism and eagerness to learn.

Equipped with a strong foundation in cybersecurity and software engineering thanks to my experience working as a Threat Intelligence Analyst for The Cyber Defense and Intelligence Center as well as a Software Engineering fellow for Headstarter AI. Additional data science and research skills developed through my time as a Research student assistant at CSUF and volunteer research for The US Department of Energy.

Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills through my work as the Communications Lead at CSUF's DIRC, Director of Marketing for the Finance Association, and Server in the restaurant industry . Strong financial analysis and valuation fundamentals through the elected role of Treasurer for the Engineering and Computer Science Inter-Club Council and as an Equity Analyst for the Student Managed Investment Fund.

I'm always open to connecting with like-minded professionals, cybersecurity experts/enthusiasts, and anyone interested in making the digital world a safer place. Always open for a coffee chat!
